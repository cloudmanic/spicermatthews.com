Options Cafe

Product

Options Cafe offers a comprehensive "Zero to Hero" options trading course designed to teach traders how to successfully trade options to earn monthly income. The course includes 14+ hours of lectures across 8 modules covering options fundamentals, strategies, and the Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta, Rho).

With over 1,200 students and a library of 2,384 shared trades for analysis, the platform provides access to actual executed trades with documented returns. Strategies covered include the Wheel Strategy and SPY Put Credit Spreads, with proven track records of 54% to 265%+ returns.

Skyclerk

Product

Skyclerk is a cloud-based bookkeeping platform that has served entrepreneurs for over a decade. The service simplifies financial management through an intuitive dashboard and automated tools, including the "Snap!Clerk" mobile app that converts receipts into digital ledger entries.

Features include unlimited contacts, storage, and ledger entries, up to 100 monthly receipt uploads, custom financial reports and graphs, and unlimited user access for sharing with team members. All for just $6/month with no additional charges for extra users or features.

Tab for a Cause

Founding Engineer

Tab for a Cause is a browser extension that raises money for charity every time you open a new tab. By displaying a small ad on your new tab page, the platform generates revenue that is donated to nonprofits chosen by the community. It's a simple, effortless way to make a difference.

As a founding engineer, I helped build the initial platform that has since raised millions of dollars for causes like education, clean water, hunger relief, and environmental conservation. The extension has been installed by hundreds of thousands of users who collectively open millions of tabs each day.

Oregon Past Due Rent

Product

Oregon Past Due Rent Notices is a legal document service designed specifically for Oregon landlords and property managers. The service issues 10-day or 13-day nonpayment notices that fully comply with Oregon law, with automatic calculation of deadline dates accounting for weekends and holidays.

At a flat rate of $15 per notice with no subscription fees, the service handles everything from printing to mailing to tracking. Same-day mailing is available for submissions received before 2 PM PT, with professional delivery and proof of mailing documentation suitable for court proceedings.
